Introducing Classic Butter Cookies — Our Best-Selling FMCG Product

Our Classic Butter Cookies have quickly become our best-selling product, loved by retailers and consumers alike across Uttar Pradesh and beyond. Baked fresh using traditional recipes and pure butter, these cookies offer a soft, crisp, melt-in-mouth texture that customers keep coming back for.

Available in three variants — Classic Butter (₹180), Chocolate Chip (₹200), and Almond Crunch (₹210) — each pack of 12 comes in a retail-ready resealable pouch. Wholesale pricing starts at ₹140/pack for orders of 200+ packs.
